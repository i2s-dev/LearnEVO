WO-K-K Edit Posted DC Labor

 

Purpose of Program

Use this program to reverse and post corrections to labor that has been entered and posted through the DC module.

General Program Operation

Limit the list of transactions to select from by entering a Work Order number, Employee number and/or Date.  A list of labor transactions matching the specified values will be displayed.  Highlight the desired entry and press Enter.  The selected entry will be displayed.  Click the Reverse button to reverse the entry.  The data will then be displayed on  the screen and available for editing and then re-posting.   Since there may be a quantity change involved, the reversal will also reverse the backflushing of components associated with the sequence and then reposting will backflush again.  If components that were backflushed were Lot and/or Serial controlled and there are multiple entries the same day making it impossible to determine which material transactions apply, you will be advised to make any material issue corrections manually in WO-G.
