RO-J-E Print QC Codes

RO-J-E  Print QC Codes

Purpose of Program

Use this program to get a listing of QC codes and descriptions.

General Program Operation

Enter a from/thru range of QC codes by typing in the codes or selecting codes from a pop-up window by pressing the F2 key (or clicking on the Lookup button).
