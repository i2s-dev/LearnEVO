US-E Update PO Electronic Signature Info

 

Enter your Employee Number and Password for Electronic PO Approval.  Once the correct password has been entered, you will be able to update the password, location of the digital signature image file and PO "Entered By" Initials.

 

To update the password, click the Reset button in the lower riught corner.  To update anything else, edit the information onthe screen.
